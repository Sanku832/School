OUR CURRICULUM

Comprehensive Education

At Chhorepatan Secondary School, we offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to nurture intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and personal growth.

Our Educational Approach

Our curriculum is designed to provide a balanced education that develops not only academic knowledge but also essential life skills, character, and values. We follow the National Curriculum Framework while incorporating innovative teaching methodologies to enhance learning outcomes.

Academic Excellence

Rigorous academic programs that challenge students and prepare them for higher education.

Student-Centered Learning

Interactive teaching methods that engage students and promote active participation.

Holistic Development

Focus on intellectual, physical, social, and emotional aspects of student development.

OUR PROGRAMS

Academic Levels

We offer comprehensive educational programs from primary to secondary levels, designed to meet the diverse needs of our students.

Primary Level (Grades 1-5)

Our primary education program focuses on building a strong foundation for lifelong learning. We create a nurturing environment where young learners develop fundamental skills, curiosity, and a love for learning.

Curriculum Highlights:

  • Foundational literacy and numeracy skills
  • Nepali and English language development
  • Basic science and social studies
  • Creative arts and expression
  • Physical education and health
  • Moral education and values

Teaching Approach:

Our primary level employs play-based and experiential learning approaches, making education engaging and enjoyable for young learners. Small class sizes ensure personalized attention for each student.

Lower Secondary Level (Grades 6-8)

Our lower secondary program builds on the primary foundation, introducing more advanced concepts and developing critical thinking skills. This is a crucial transitional phase where students begin to explore their interests and aptitudes.

Curriculum Highlights:

  • Advanced language arts (Nepali and English)
  • Mathematics (algebra, geometry, statistics)
  • Integrated science (physics, chemistry, biology)
  • Social studies and civics
  • Computer science and digital literacy
  • Health and physical education
  • Arts, music, and optional subjects

Teaching Approach:

Our lower secondary program employs inquiry-based learning and project-based approaches to develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration skills. Students are encouraged to take more responsibility for their learning.

Secondary Level (Grades 9-10)

Our secondary education program prepares students for the Secondary Education Examination (SEE) and lays the groundwork for higher education. We focus on academic rigor while continuing to develop character and life skills.

Curriculum Highlights:

  • Compulsory subjects: Nepali, English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies
  • Optional subjects: Computer Science, Accounting, Health & Physical Education
  • Advanced science (physics, chemistry, biology)
  • Career counseling and guidance
  • SEE preparation and practice examinations
  • Research projects and practical applications

Teaching Approach:

Our secondary program balances exam preparation with deeper conceptual understanding. We employ a combination of lectures, discussions, laboratory work, and project-based learning to ensure comprehensive preparation for SEE and beyond.

Admission Process

Ready to provide your child with an exceptional educational experience? Follow these steps to apply for admission to Chhorepatan Secondary School.

1

Submit Application

Complete the online application form or visit our admission office to obtain and submit a physical application.

2

Entrance Assessment

Students will take an entrance assessment to determine their academic level and learning needs.

3

Interview

Parents and students will be invited for an interview with school administrators or faculty members.

4

Admission Decision

Admission decisions will be communicated to parents within two weeks of completing the process.
