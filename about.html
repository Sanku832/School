OUR HISTORY

Excellence in Education Since 2021

Chhorepatan Secondary School was established in 2021 with a vision to provide quality education to the students of Pokhara and surrounding areas. What started as a small institution has now grown into a reputable educational establishment known for academic excellence and holistic development.

The school was founded by a group of dedicated educators and community leaders who recognized the need for a modern educational institution that combines traditional values with contemporary teaching methods.

Over the years, we have expanded our facilities, enhanced our curriculum, and built a team of highly qualified teachers committed to nurturing the potential of every student.

Our Motto

"Knowledge, Character, Excellence"

OUR PURPOSE

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive everything we do at Chhorepatan Secondary School.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality education that empowers students to become lifelong learners, critical thinkers, and responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

We strive to create a nurturing and inclusive learning environment where students can discover and develop their unique talents and abilities.

Our Vision

We envision Chhorepatan Secondary School as a center of educational excellence that prepares students to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Our vision is to be recognized as a leading educational institution that balances academic rigor with character development, producing graduates who are not only knowledgeable but also ethical, compassionate, and socially responsible.

OUR PRINCIPLES

Our Core Values

These values guide our approach to education and shape our school culture.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all aspects of education, encouraging students to pursue their highest potential in academics, sports, arts, and personal development.

Integrity

We value honesty, ethical behavior, and moral courage, teaching students to make principled choices and take responsibility for their actions.

Respect

We foster respect for self, others, and the environment, celebrating diversity and promoting inclusivity in our school community.

Innovation

We embrace creativity, critical thinking, and innovation, preparing students to adapt to a rapidly changing world and find solutions to complex problems.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of teamwork and collaboration, teaching students to work effectively with others and value diverse perspectives.

Social Responsibility

We encourage students to be socially responsible citizens who contribute positively to their communities and work towards the greater good.

OUR PRIDE

Our Achievements

We take pride in the accomplishments of our students and our institution.

Academic Excellence

95% pass rate in SEE examinations with 40% students scoring A+ grades.

Sports Championships

District champions in football, volleyball, and athletics competitions.

Science Innovations

First prize in the National Science Exhibition for two consecutive years.

Community Service

Recognized for outstanding community service initiatives and environmental projects.
