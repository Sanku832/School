FIND US

Our Location

We are conveniently located in Chhorepatan, Pokhara. Visit us to explore our campus and facilities.

Getting Here

By Bus

Take any city bus heading to Chhorepatan and get off at Chhorepatan Chowk. The school is a 5-minute walk from there.

By Taxi

Taxis are readily available throughout Pokhara. Ask the driver to take you to Chhorepatan Secondary School.

Parking

Visitor parking is available on campus. Please check in at the security gate upon arrival.
