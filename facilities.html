WORLD-CLASS INFRASTRUCTURE

Modern Learning Environment

At Chhorepatan Secondary School, we provide state-of-the-art facilities to create an optimal learning environment that fosters academic excellence and holistic development.

Our Campus

Our sprawling campus is designed to provide a safe, stimulating, and conducive environment for learning and growth. Spread across 5 acres of land, our campus features modern buildings, spacious classrooms, specialized laboratories, sports facilities, and green spaces.

Modern Infrastructure

Contemporary buildings designed with student safety and comfort in mind.

Technology Integration

Campus-wide Wi-Fi, smart classrooms, and digital learning resources.

Green Environment

Landscaped gardens, play areas, and eco-friendly practices.

ACADEMIC FACILITIES

Learning Spaces

Our academic facilities are designed to provide an optimal learning environment that fosters curiosity, critical thinking, and academic excellence.

Modern Classrooms

Our spacious, well-ventilated classrooms are equipped with modern furniture, smart boards, and audio-visual equipment to enhance the learning experience.

  • Smart boards and projectors
  • Ergonomic furniture
  • Optimal lighting and ventilation

Science Laboratories

Our well-equipped science laboratories provide students with hands-on learning experiences in physics, chemistry, and biology.

  • Modern lab equipment
  • Safety protocols and equipment
  • Qualified lab assistants

Computer Laboratory

Our state-of-the-art computer lab is equipped with the latest hardware and software to develop digital literacy and computational thinking skills.

  • High-speed internet connection
  • Latest software and hardware
  • One computer per student policy

Library & Resource Center

Our well-stocked library offers a wide range of books, periodicals, and digital resources to foster a love for reading and research.

  • Extensive collection of books
  • Digital resources and e-books
  • Quiet study spaces

Art & Craft Studio

Our art studio provides a creative space for students to explore various art forms and develop their artistic talents.

  • Art supplies and materials
  • Display area for student artwork
  • Specialized art equipment

Music Room

Our music room is equipped with various musical instruments and audio equipment to nurture musical talents and appreciation.

  • Various musical instruments
  • Sound-proof practice rooms
  • Recording equipment

SPORTS FACILITIES

Physical Development

We believe in the importance of physical education and sports in the holistic development of students. Our school offers excellent sports facilities to encourage physical fitness and sportsmanship.

Sports Field

Our spacious sports field provides ample space for various outdoor sports and physical activities.

  • Football field
  • Cricket pitch
  • Athletics track
  • Outdoor basketball court

Indoor Sports Facilities

Our indoor sports facilities allow students to engage in various indoor sports and physical activities regardless of weather conditions.

  • Badminton courts
  • Table tennis tables
  • Chess and carrom boards
  • Yoga and aerobics space

Fitness Center

Our fitness center is equipped with various exercise equipment to promote physical fitness and a healthy lifestyle among students and staff.

  • Cardio equipment
  • Weight training equipment
  • Qualified fitness instructors
  • Personalized fitness programs

"Physical fitness is not only one of the most important keys to a healthy body, it is the basis of dynamic and creative intellectual activity." - John F. Kennedy

ADDITIONAL FACILITIES

Student Support Services

We provide various support services to ensure the well-being, safety, and comfort of our students during their time at school.

Cafeteria

Our cafeteria serves nutritious and balanced meals prepared in a hygienic environment. We offer a variety of food options to cater to different dietary preferences and requirements.

Health Center

Our health center is staffed with qualified medical personnel to provide first aid and basic medical care. We conduct regular health check-ups and maintain health records of all students.

Counseling Center

Our counseling center provides academic, career, and personal counseling to help students navigate challenges and make informed decisions about their future.

Transportation

We provide safe and reliable transportation services for students with GPS-tracked buses and trained drivers. Our buses cover various routes across the city.

Auditorium

Our spacious auditorium is equipped with modern audio-visual systems and seating arrangements for school assemblies, cultural programs, and other events.

Hostel Facilities

We provide comfortable and secure hostel facilities for students from distant locations. Our hostels are supervised by experienced wardens and staff.
