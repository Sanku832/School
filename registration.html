ADMISSION PROCESS

How to Apply

Follow these steps to apply for admission to Chhorepatan Secondary School for the 2024 academic year.

1

Submit Application

Complete the online application form or visit our admission office to obtain and submit a physical application.

2

Entrance Assessment

Students will take an entrance assessment to determine their academic level and learning needs.

3

Interview

Parents and students will be invited for an interview with school administrators or faculty members.

4

Admission Decision

Admission decisions will be communicated to parents within two weeks of completing the process.

Important Information

Applications for the 2024 academic year are now open. The deadline for submission is November 30, 2023. Entrance assessments will be conducted from December 5 to December 15, 2023.

For more information, please contact our Admissions Office at +977-61-123457 or email us at admissions@chhorepatan.edu.np.

FEE STRUCTURE

Academic Year 2024

Below is the fee structure for the 2024 academic year. Please note that fees are subject to change.

Grades 1-5

Admission Fee Rs. 5,000
Annual Fee Rs. 10,000
Monthly Tuition Fee Rs. 2,500
Computer Lab Fee Rs. 1,000
Library Fee Rs. 500
Total Annual Cost Rs. 46,500

*Excluding transportation and uniform costs

Grades 6-8

Annual Fee Rs. 12,000

Science Lab Fee Rs. 1,000
Total Annual Cost Rs. 56,500

Grades 9-10

Additional Information

  • Fees can be paid annually, bi-annually, or monthly as per convenience.
  • Sibling discount of 10% on tuition fees is available for the second child and 15% for the third child.
  • Transportation fees vary depending on the distance and route. Please contact the administrative office for details.
  • Scholarships are available for meritorious and economically disadvantaged students. Please inquire at the admission office.
